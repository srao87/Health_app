Weekly engine

How to read this: your scale weight bounces daily from water and food, so the app reads the smoothed 7-day trend, not the raw number. Each week it back-calculates your true maintenance from what you ate plus the weight you lost, then sets the next target.

Settings

A word on 0.75 kg/week. It's a fast pace — fine if you have fat to spare, but it can drain energy and muscle if held too long. The floor is your guardrail: the engine never recommends below it. Treat the floor as a hard stop, not a goal, and if the cut feels rough, drop the pace to 0.5 and let your target rise. Not medical advice — set the floor with your own body, and a doctor or dietitian, in mind.
